## SECTION 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:20 – 0:50)

> **[SCREEN: Stay on the landing page, scroll slowly through the hero section]**

**NARRATION:**
> "Most small pharmacies still manage everything manually — notebooks, Excel files, or nothing at all.
>
> This leads to slow checkout at the counter because staff have to compute totals by hand.
> Stock levels are unknown until a medicine completely runs out.
> Expired medicines go unnoticed until a customer complains.
> And there is no easy way to check how much the store earned today.
>
> These are real, everyday problems — and MediTrack was built to solve all of them."

## SECTION 3 — THE SOLUTION (0:50 – 1:20)

> **[SCREEN: Scroll down to the features section and medicine preview cards on the landing page]**

**NARRATION:**
> "MediTrack is a web-based Point-of-Sale system that works on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile — with no installation required.
>
> It has two user roles:
>
> The **Admin** — who sets up the store, manages the medicine inventory, monitors stock and expiry dates, creates seller accounts, and views full sales reports.
>
> The **Seller** — who operates the front counter, searches for medicines, processes sales, and generates receipts.
>
> Everything is real-time. Any update the admin makes is immediately visible to the seller."

**NARRATION:**

## RECORDING TIPS

- **Before recording:** Log out first so the video starts clean at the landing page
- **Browser:** Chrome, zoom at 90%, full screen or at least 1280px wide
- **Exact order:** Landing → Register → Admin login → Dashboard → Medicines (add) → Accounts (create seller) → Log out → Seller login → Dashboard → Cart → Receipt → Seller Sales → Log out → Admin login → Admin Sales → Landing (closing)
- **Pace:** Speak slower than feels natural — viewers need time to follow along
- **Loom:** Enable camera bubble for the intro and closing; mic only for the demo sections
